Jeremy Bailer

Jeremy Bailer is a film producer and founder of Madrose Productions.

Jeremy Bailer is an award-winning film producer who has been financing and producing independent films for over 15 years. His films have won major honors at the Sundance Film Festival and the Berlin International Film Festival, including Robot & Frank and Arcadia, respectively. He received an Emmy nomination for Outstanding Historical Documentary for his work on Team Foxcatcher (Netflix). Along with Team Foxcatcher, Angle (Peacock) is another documentary film where the sport of wrestling is the backdrop to incedbile human stories and events. Prior to his film career, Bailer was a Division I wrestler at the University of Pennsylvania and graduated with a BA in Communications and Economics in 1998.
